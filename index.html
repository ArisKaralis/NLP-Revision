Welcome to NLP Fundamentals

This site explores fundamental methodologies within both classical and neural Natural Language Processing (NLP). These techniques form a critical underpinning for understanding how machines process and understand human language.

Note for AI Master's Students: Understanding these classic and neural techniques provides a strong foundation for several reasons:

  • Conceptual Building Blocks: Many modern NLP models have roots in or address limitations of earlier methods. Knowing the history helps in understanding the "why" behind current architectures.
  • Baselines & Solutions: For certain tasks or with limited data, classic techniques can still be effective. Neural models offer state-of-the-art performance on complex tasks.
  • Feature Engineering Insights: Understanding manual feature engineering in classic NLP can provide intuition for neural network inputs. Neural models often learn features automatically.
  • Scalability Understanding: Both classic and neural techniques have considerations for scaling to large datasets and models.
